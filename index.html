License Reinstatement Program (LRP)

Regain your driving privileges while you pay down traffic fines.

  • Purpose: Reinstate a suspended driver's license for non-payment of traffic tickets.
  • Eligibility: Tickets or cases must be ≥ 60 days old; valid auto insurance required.
  • Scope: May include suspended tickets from other Iowa counties.
  • License Status: Yes — lifts suspension once approved; max 5 LRP plans in a lifetime.
  • Civil Penalties: Must be paid in full before enrolling.

County Attorney Payment Plan

Stay current on court debt, remove registration holds, and avoid garnishments.

  • Purpose: Satisfy probation/parole requirements, address registration holds, prevent wage garnishments.
  • Eligibility: Cases must be ≥ 30 days old and total balance ≥ $300.
  • Scope: Polk County criminal & traffic cases only (no out-of-county debt).
  • License Status: Does not affect driver's license suspension.
  • Delinquency: Plan must be current to avoid legal action.
